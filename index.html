past work

once upon another time

this is a cover of once upon another time by sara bareilles. i arranged it for my group at connecticut college, vox cameli, and recorded all the parts at home using my own voice.

created in reaper. june 2018.

alive mash-up

what started as arranging practice ended up becoming a larger project - one that focused more on production but also mashed-up alive by sia and multiple songs by the staves (blood i bled, outlaw). all the vocal parts are recorded with my own voice.

created in reaper. july 2018.

i go crazy

this was created for a cover contest of i go crazy by orla gartland that i took up as extra incentive and purpose for practicing my production skills. all the vocal parts are recorded with my own voice.

created in reaper. july 2018.

crush

this was created for a cover contest of crush by tessa violet. i decided to follow the idea i had for my interpretation of it and incorporate guitar so i could get in some instrumental production practice with my equipment. all the parts are peformed by me.

created in reaper. july 2018.

death with dignity

i was obsessively listening to this song, and then i saw thirdstory's cover of it. it inspired me to produce a version of it with a 3-part harmony as well, but i wanted to challenge myself to make this mini-cover with a different style. all the parts are performed by me.

created in reaper. august 2018.
