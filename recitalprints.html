madeline grey

i am a multi-disciplinary artist - graphic designer, front-end web developer, producer, mixer, and a cappella arranger.

musical recital prints

i created a series of prints for my sister's clarinet recital, who approached me to create advertisement posters

as a former college student, and music student, whose a cappella groups did at least one poster campaign for our concerts each semester, i already knew the important information that needed to be relayed. but i also recognized that senior recitals generally had a different tone than the meme-filled performance posters of other events. also, this was a particularly important recital for her for a multitude of reasons, so i wanted to elevate them to her liking.

i've taken pictures that play with exposure and light before, so i got my DSLR and took a whole host of mid-range shots and close-ups with different exposures in different lighting (fairy lights, spotlight - even a suncatcher!) i made various compositions of these images first and then based the rest of the design around them.
